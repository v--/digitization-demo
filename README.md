# Digitization demo

Over the years I have constructed my own workflow for scanning and digitizing books, mostly math books. For an example of the result, see [Никола Обрешков - Висша Алгебра](<https://ivasilev.net/files/Сканирани/Никола Обрешков - Висша алгебра.pdf>) (5th edition from 1962).

A reproducible demonstration of my digitization workflow on a book requires uploading the raw scanned images, which is impractical. I will instead use as an example a paper of von Neumann --- see the subdirectory ["John von Neumann - On the Theory of Games of Strategy"](<./John von Neumann - On the Theory of Games of Strategy>).
